Corporate

We were born in 2014 as a social project with artisans from Guerrero. In 2017 we evolved to be the commercial arm of artisans, farmers, small producers and social entrepreneurs to reach international markets in a professional and appropriate way.

From the agricultural and artisanal sector we export certified Mexican products and develop innovative products for new international markets. We also carry out the necessary export logistics operations to successfully deliver goods to the desired destination port.

Added Value

Our added value is to advise the producer on the total adaptation of the product so that it achieves the requirements and quality of the destination market.

  • Packaging adaptation.
  • Formulation and presentation.
  • Labeling and buyer requirements.
  • Commercial and technical documentation.

Social Responsibility

The linking with Civil Society Organizations is key to articulate and promote actions to support sectors with limited resources or in conditions susceptible to marginalization and poverty.

During these years MW Company has collaborated with different foundations to achieve a more equitable, supportive and prosperous society. Our business vision goes hand in hand with the commitment to build a common good for our country.
